AI Reflection

During this project, I used AI as a support tool for development, debugging, and documentation.

Rather than asking AI to generate the entire task as a single copy-paste solution, I approached the work in smaller steps. I divided the project into manageable parts and asked focused questions about specific problems, such as backend structure, Oracle integration, validation logic, or small frontend/UI changes.

The main areas where AI was used include:
- understanding and structuring parts of the FastAPI backend
- connecting the Oracle database to the backend
- refining frontend template updates with Jinja2, HTML, and CSS
- converting manual ID-based form inputs into dropdown-based selections
- improving validation logic and user-facing error handling
- reviewing small UI/UX improvements

All final decisions, integration steps, and testing were performed by me.  
I reviewed the suggestions, adapted them to my own project structure, and verified the behavior by running the application and testing the relevant endpoints and frontend pages.

AI was used as a development assistant, not as a replacement for implementation or understanding.  
The final submitted version reflects my own work in combining, adjusting, and testing the suggested ideas step by step.
